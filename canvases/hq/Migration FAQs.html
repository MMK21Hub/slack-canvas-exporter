How will authentication work?

Previously, many Hack Club apps relied on Slack's OAuth to work. Going forward, most apps will be switching to some combination of the Identity Vault (IDV, identity.hackclub.com) and email.

​

Will I have to reverify my identity?

No, you almost certainly won't have to! The current identity system is authenticated through email, and it will stay that way.
