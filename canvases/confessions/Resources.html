:sparkling-blahaj-heart: Resources

For anyone having a rough time and/or having thoughts of suicide. if you'd like, and I really encourage you to, talk to someone. Do something to make your feelings heard more. You don't have to share everything, just what you want. you are valid no matter what and deserve to be happy. :blahaj-heart: you can get through whatever it is

  • Talk to a friend
  • Talk to a trusted adult (such as a teacher, guidance counselor, or social worker)
  • Visit an in-person youth hub

What to Expect When Calling a Hotline: We maintain an FAQ about hotlines and what usually does and doesn't happen when you call one.

Worldwide Directories

We know of two international lists that are maintained by reputable agencies; try these if you don't find what you're looking for below:

  • The International Association for Suicide Prevention's partnership with Find a Helpline
  • The Befrienders maintain a hotline database; use the "Find a Helpline by Country" control at the top of their page.

Do be aware that most global hotline lists are maintained privately by members of the public. Although these people generally mean well, the information on their sites is rarely accurate or up-to-date.
